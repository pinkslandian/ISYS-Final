How things are rated

When I critiqued the subjects on my webpage I judged by: Clarity, Content, and Creativity . Then I took qualities like Terrible, Pretty Good, and Excellent and assigned them numbers on a scale of 1 to 9. After judging by each quality, I added each number and assigned them to an overall score.

Grading Qualities Scores
Clarity Terrible 1-3
Content Pretty Good 4-6
Creativity Excellent 7-9
